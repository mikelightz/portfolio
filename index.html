About Me

Michael Edwards Jr is a full-stack web developer from Virginia Beach, VA and currently based in Charlotte, NC. He is one part of The Social Contract music and art collective.

More About Me

As a self-taught junior software developer, Michael is passionate about learning and expanding his skills in software development. With a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, & Javascript, he is eager to tackle new challenges and make a positive impact in the industry. Michael has shown strong self-discipline and resilience on his way to earning several certifications through online courses to gain a comprehensive understanding of software development principles and practices. During his training, he worked on a local coffee shop's beer & wine menu to develop his programming skills, which helped him to gain major practical experience in developing software solutions. Michael is proficient in front and back-end development, and is eager to continue building his knowledge with SQL and data analytics. He builds primarily with the React library & Bootstrap framework although he has experience with Angular and Typescript.

As exhibited by his multiple leadership positions in various customer service jobs, Michael is great at relationship building & comfortable working in a collaborative team environment. Currently, Michael is seeking an entry-level software development position to further his skills and contribute to the success of a software development team. He is excited to apply his knowledge and skills to develop software solutions that meet the needs of clients and users. Michael holds a BA in Film Studies from the University of North Carolina at Wilmington where he looks to sound design for films. In his free time, he enjoys music production, yoga & exercise, reading, and outdoor hikes. He is committed to continuous learning and improvement through participation in online communities and attending industry events. As a motivated and enthusiastic junior software developer, Michael is eager to make a meaningful contribution to the field of software development and become a valuable asset to any team.
